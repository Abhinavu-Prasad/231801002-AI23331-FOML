# Conexxa
Conexxa is a mobile app that connects students with personalized mentorship, internship, and job opportunities. By leveraging advanced AI-driven matching, Conexxa helps students build meaningful connections, explore diverse career paths, and unlock their full potential.


Key Features:

Student Profiles: Showcase your skills, interests, and goals
Smart Matching: Intelligent algorithms find the best-fit opportunities
Mentorship Programs: Connect with experienced professionals
Internship & Job Search: Access curated openings at partner companies
Progress Tracking: Monitor your achievements and receive recommendations

Conexxa empowers students to navigate the job market with confidence, explore fulfilling career paths, and kickstart their professional journey.
